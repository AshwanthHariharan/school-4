what's new

  • British Council International Dimension in Schools 2019-2022

    The International Dimension in Schools has been awarded to the schools that have shown a commitment to embedding international awareness and understanding within their class or school. It is achieved by completing collaborative, curriculum-based work with a number of international partner schools and involvement of the wider community.

  • Behtar India Campaign - Top performing school in the city-Won a chance to host the Behtar India Go Green Summit in Chennai

    We are proud to inform that our school is the top performing school in the city and won a chance to host the summit. It was an initiative that aims in bringing together schools, students, corporate and citizens towards discussing the problems and solutions with an aim to build a sustainable environment and future. Top corporate officials, individuals, and change-makers will come together to discuss, brainstorm and churn out relevant solutions to make the world a better place to live in. The Behtar India Go Green City Summit has been held in Chennai on the 10th of October.
